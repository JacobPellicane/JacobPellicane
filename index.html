Denxel's baked goods

At the tender age of ten, Denzel's grandmother helped him bake his first batch of chocolate chip cookies, which is how he fell in love with baking. He was fascinated with the pleasure of making something tasty from scratch. He refined his abilities throughout the years by trying out several recipes and even coming up with a few of his own. His goal is to spread the love of baking to as many people as possible and encourage them to make their own delicious treats.

Almond Croissants: A delicate pastry filled with the sweet nutty goodness of almond paste, dusted with powdered sugar, and baked to a golden perfection Ingredients: 1 sheet of puff pastry, 1/2 cup of almond paste, 1/4 cup of powdered sugar, 1 egg (for egg wash). Instructions: First, preheat the oven to 375°F. Next, roll out the puff pastry and cut it into triangles. Spread a thin layer of almond paste on each triangle. Then, roll each triangle from the base to the tip and curve into a crescent shape. Finally, brush with egg wash and sprinkle with powdered sugar, and bake for 15-20 minutes until it’s golden brown
